ABOUT US

Texpert, established in 2001, is a chemical manufacturer that offers products to the leather, paper, hygiene, and automotive sectors. Our team, originating from within the industry, develops products that provide solutions to the challenges faced by textile enterprises. We address issues such as fiber, settlement, business, machinery, paint, and chemical quality as a whole, offering comprehensive solutions to prevent clogging and other operational problems.

Our Mission

In the unique structure of the Turkish textile sector, Texpert aims to focus on quality and cost elements that pave the way for businesses, bringing a new perspective that solves problems. We strive to be a partner that works shoulder to shoulder with the sector, driving towards solutions with a purpose-driven approach.

Our Vision

In Turkey, Texpert aims to be the leading problem solver in the industry, developing and guiding with respect for the environment and business ethics. We are committed to defending these principles in all our operations.
