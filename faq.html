Frequently Asked Questions

What products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of products including printing products, dyeing products, pre-operation products, finishing products, and various other products to meet different industrial needs.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by visiting our products page and selecting the items you need. For large or custom orders, please contact our sales team through the contact page.

Do you provide international shipping?

Yes, we provide international shipping to various countries. Shipping costs and delivery times may vary depending on the destination. Please contact us for more details.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase if the product is unused and in its original packaging. Please refer to our return policy page for more information or contact our customer service team.

Can I get a product sample?

Yes, we offer samples for some of our products. Please contact our sales team to request a sample and provide details of the product you are interested in.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via the contact page on our website. We are available to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.
